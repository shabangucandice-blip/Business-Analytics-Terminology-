# Business-Analytics-Terminology-
BrightLearn weekly exercises 


A great analyst starts by mastering the language of the field. This repository contains a comprehensive breakdown of 38 essential terms used across Business Analytics, covering foundational concepts, data types, and the metrics that drive marketing campaign success.

 Learning Objectives
By exploring this project, I will be able to:
Speak the Language: Identify and correctly use the core vocabulary of Business and Data Analytics.
Clarify Confusion: Distinguish between commonly confused terms (e.g., Metric vs. KPI, or CAC vs. CLV).
Measure Success: Recognise the key performance indicators (KPIs) used to evaluate marketing and business performance. 

 What’s Inside
The terminology is organised into four critical pillars: 
Foundational Concepts: The "big picture" terms that define the industry.
Types of Data: Understanding the building blocks of any analysis.
Core KPIs: The vital signs of a healthy business.
Marketing Campaign Analytics: Specific metrics used to track and optimise growth. 

Why this matters: Data is just noise without the right context. Mastering these terms ensures that when you're in the room with stakeholders, you aren't just reporting numbers—you’re telling a story that the business understands.
